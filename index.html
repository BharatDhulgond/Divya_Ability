About DivyaAbility Foundation

Light in Shadows – because true inclusion means seeing strength where others see limits.

Founded in February 2024, DivyaAbility Foundation doesn’t just support; we shift perceptions.
As a Section 8 non-profit registered under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India, we craft pathways that turn awareness into action — empowering people with disabilities to learn, lead, and belong.
