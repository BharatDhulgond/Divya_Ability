Our Impact

Turning Purpose into Measurable Progress

At DivyaAbility Foundation, inclusion isn’t just a goal — it’s a measurable journey. Through knowledge, access, and action, we continue to unlock the potential within the disability community across India.

Highlights That Define Our Work:

  • 500+ lives advanced through courses, mentoring, and workshops
  • 20+ states presence — inclusion without boundaries
  • 10+ curated courses building real-world confidence and capability
  • 10+ powerful webinars centering lived experiences
  • 20+ committed volunteers turning intention into everyday action

Upskilling with Purpose: Our Courses

We’ve built a library of over 10 inclusive, career-aligned courses that continue to reach individuals from under-resourced and unskilled backgrounds, transforming knowledge into agency.

Competitive Fundamentals of Maths & Reasoning

Learners master concepts in a screen-reader-friendly format, confidently navigating exams and reshaping perceptions about disability and numerical thinking.

Accessible Maths with LaTeX

Participants engage with mathematical content using assistive technology, bringing academic writing into inclusive spaces with precision and ease.

Stock Market: Fundamental & Technical Analysis

Individuals gain insight into market trends and strategy, confidently participating in financial decision-making and long-term planning.

ExcelPro Course

Participants navigate spreadsheets, manage data, and streamline tasks — powering their workspaces and daily life with independence and clarity.

Awareness Through Real Conversations

Our webinars spotlight key conversations around disability rights, inclusion, mental well-being, and accessible tools — igniting curiosity, understanding, and collaboration across communities.

Scribe Access That Levels the Field

We coordinate scribe support for academic and competitive exams across the states we serve, bridging a crucial gap and ensuring seamless exam readiness for visually impaired candidates.
