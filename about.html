Who Are We?

Where others see limits, we see potential

Founded in February 2024, DivyaAbility Foundation is a registered Section 8 non-profit organization in India dedicated to empowering people with disabilities.

We transform lives by making disability inclusion real — through inclusive education, digital skills training, mentorship, scholarships, and community programs.

We believe true inclusion goes beyond physical access — it means changing perceptions, creating opportunities, and empowering every person to learn, grow, and lead.

Our Vision

A world where people with disabilities are not only included, but truly empowered to shape their own futures and inspire change.

Our Mission

Designing inclusive education programs and digital skill-building workshops
Offering scholarships for disabled students, ensuring education without financial barriers
Providing personalised support services that help learners study independently and confidently
Advocating for a society where empowerment replaces sympathy

What Makes Us Unique

  • We turn awareness into action, not just words
  • Every course and service is built around the real needs and voices of the disability community
  • We focus on skill development and confidence, giving learners the tools to thrive in study, work, and daily life
  • At DivyaAbility, inclusion isn’t a checkbox — it’s at the heart of everything we do.
